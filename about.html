    • Most of Riverdale is based off of the Archie Comic Books. Riverdale has characters like Josie and The Pussy cats which are also own by Archie Comics.

    Jingle Jangle

    Jingle Jangle is a drug that goes around in Season 2 of Riverdale, its said to be passed around by the gang the Goulies- rival gang of the infamous Southside Serpents.

    Jason Blossom and Polly Cooper

    This shows Polly Copper and Jason Blossom.Jason Blossom was "mysteriously" murdered leaving pregnant Polly to be sent away to a "loony bin".Jason is the twin brother of Cheryl Blossom and Polly is the older sister of Elizabeth "Betty" Cooper. Later in season 1 of Riverdale we find out that Jason and Polly are second cousins.

    Bughead

    What is a Bughead you might be thinking , well its the relationship between girl next door Betty Copper and outcast Jughead Jones.

    B and V

    B and V is the tag team of bestfriends Veronica and Betty. Even though in season two there friendship is put to the test they are still going strong, even a few out there ship them as a couple which may not happen seeing neither of them has come out with any feelings after kiss in season one.
